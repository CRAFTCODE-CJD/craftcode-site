**Sprite Optimizer** — a professional Paper2D plugin for Unreal Engine. Trims
transparent edges, packs textures into atlases, auto-generates Material
Instances, and lets you reverse-engineer finished atlases — all in one
interactive editor with 50-level undo/redo.

> Full Russian docs are being migrated. See [the Russian version](/plugins/sprite-optimizer/)
> for the current content or [open the FAB listing](https://www.fab.com/listings/d467cf90-8576-4aa9-b55d-285060a5d1bf).

## Key features

- **One build, 9 engines** — UE 4.27 and 5.0–5.7 from a single source.
- **Pixel-perfect output** — no rescaling, no reinterpretation, 1:1.
- **Interactive atlas editor** — drag & drop from Content Browser, manual
  placement and rotation, rubber-band selection, multi-page atlases.
- **Resumable sessions** — save a session as a Project asset, double-click to
  restore everything byte-for-byte.
- **Material Instances** — auto-generated with pixel-based UV parameters,
  works in 3D and UMG.
- **50 levels of undo/redo** — experiment without fear.
- **Editor-only** — zero runtime cost, not shipped to packaged builds.
